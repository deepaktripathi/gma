Registration & Admission

Admission starts from Nursery and the minimum age for L.K.G. is 3+ years. Admission form with Prospectus can be obtained from Office on Payment in cash, G.M. Academy, Sabji Mandi Road, Subabazar, (Khorabar) Gorakhpur. You may register the name online too. Date of Interview/test will be informed on receipt of filling up from along with Admission form which is normally issued from the month of January for the session starting from April.

Parents/Guardian must be careful in writing the correct spelling of their ward and their own names and also the correct Date of Birth of the student. Once these are written in the form, these will not be changed. Submission of birth certificate is a must.

Admission is given on the basis of an admission test subject to availability of seat in the particular class. The test comprises a written examination in English, Science, General Knowledge, Hindi and Mathematics.

It is one hour duration for Primary/Junior Section, while the written test in English, Mathematics and Hindi for an hour will be there for primary section too.

Specific syllabus is prescribed for the admission test. It can be asked while processing the prospectus.

The candidate should be medically fit and should not have any physical deficiency/shortcoming which may come in the way of his/her participation in all school activity including games, sports and swimming.

Boys/Girls suffering from any contagious, constitutional or hereditary disease or infirmity are not admitted. Parents’ declaration should be given at the time of Admission.
