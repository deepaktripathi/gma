Pre-Primary Level

At Pre-Primary level, We adopt a play school theme in classes. Children are taught while playing in the guidance of expert teachers.


Primary Level

At Primary level, children are treated as individuals and their emotion and social development is a key concern for teachers. Curriculum provides a framework for a range of sensory and experimental learning as well as development of basic concepts and skills in various subjects such as Mathematics, Environmental Science, Computer Education besides two language. Painting, Craft work, Music, Yoga, Sport are provided for all round development.


Junior Level

Junior school years are recognized as a period of rapid development and transition. Curriculum is made to keep abreast of the growing “ Inner Life” of individual as well as pattern in peer relationship. Art and Craft, Sports, Music, Life skill, Moral Education, General Knowledge and Work Experience besides, Sanskrit are integral part of the School program.
Science Stream: Science, Maths, Hindi and Computer
