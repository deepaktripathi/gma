Visiting Rules

In Order to ensure continuity of academic time and opportunity, visits by local guardians or visiting parents will be restricted. However parents or local guardians or persons authorized to meet their boarders may meet them only on the 2 and 4 Sunday of the month, between 3 PM to 5PM. They are requested not to disturb the children during class hours. They should meet the ward only through their wardens.


Visitors

Parents May meet the Principal with prior appointment on the working days. The Following are the school office Hours: Summer- 07:30 am to 01:30 pm (Lunch Break 10:40 am to 11:00pm) Winter- 08:30 am to 02:30 pm (Lunch Break 11:30am to 12:15 pm) Sunday/Holidays will Office closed.
