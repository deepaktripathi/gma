Faculty

The faculty of G.M. Academy Sabji Mandi Road, Subabazar, (Khorabar) Gorakhpur, shapes its special character. Each faculty member is both a teacher and a scholar and expert in his/her own field. The faculty of G.M. Academy Gorakhpur consists of qualified teachers who have extensive teaching experience in their respective fields. All teachers are also involved in some school activity, as House Reps, Class Reps, Environment Council, Editorial Boards, Cultural Department and Sport Department. In fact all teachers also share membership with students on various committees and supervise individual or small-group research projects. The opportunities for formal and informal relationships with G.M. Academy faculty are plentiful and rewarding, resulting in lifelong friendships as well as professional mentor ship that shape student’s lives and career.
